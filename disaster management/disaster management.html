DISASTER MANAGEMENT

You may wonder "DISASTER MANAGEMENT??" Just as the name implies its about how to manage disaster. Manage in term of how to save lives/property before,during, or after a disaster occurs.

According to an survey almost 100million people have died due to disaster from 1700 a.d. till 2019 a.d. worldwide

Disaster doesnot always take lives directly! Lives lost during the occurance period of a disaster is low compared to the lives lost after the disaster happens. It may happen due to various reason such as untimely rescue, people doesnot always know what to do after a disaster occurs, etc.
